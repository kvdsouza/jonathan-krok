The Legend

The Harvard Krokodiloes

Founded in 1946 by六个 Harvard undergraduates who wanted to bring barbershop harmonies to campus. Today, they're one of the most famous collegiate a cappella groups in the world.

🎤

80+ Years Strong

Since 1946, the Kroks have been Harvard's premier all-male a cappella group, touring six continents and performing for global audiences including heads of state.

🌍

World Tour Veterans

The Kroks have performed in over 60 countries — from the Great Wall of China to the sands of Dubai. They're Harvard's musical ambassadors to the world.

🏆

A Cappella Champions

Multiple-time winners of the ICCA (International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella). Their arrangements and performances set the gold standard.
